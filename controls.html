All buttons have the following properties:

  • 1px solid border
  • Inset box shadow with 10px blur and 0 offset
  • Matching border color and inset shadow color
  • White text
  • 10px padding
  • 20px margin

Sliders

Sliders may implement a black bar and heagonal peg, round peg or square peg for position and control

Volume

Volume control shall be done with black background that will be filled with a colour to illustrate level of volume along the bar and single hexagonal peg.

Scroll bar

Scroll bars should be a solid line of white, black or grey, otherwise one of the colors from the color palette should be used.

To my knowledge, browser scrollbars can't be styled with CSS, only with some tricky JS. - MM

Switches

This section is pending review.

The switch comes in three sizes:

.switch-large, .switch-small, .switch-xsmall

JS toggles the ".on" and ".off" classes of the ancestor ".switch".

".switch" has 2 descendants:
".switch-plate" which is the housing that changes color, and
".switch-control" which is the knob that slides.

The switch class is set to ".off" by default.
