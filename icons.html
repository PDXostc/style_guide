Icons▲

This section is work-in-progress. It will be updated with a visual library of icon images and a grid system for building icons.

Icons shall be solid white, solid black or outlined chevrons

If outlined is used, an internal glow/shadow shall be used that contrasts the box.

If the Box is shadowed, the chevron shall be white or outlined with contrasting colour.

If the Box is solid coloured, the chevron shall be white or black only
